About

Welcome to The BirdCage San Francisco, a unique appointment-only store that offers a cozy and creative space for local art enthusiasts, coffee aficionados, and tea lovers. Our store features a curated selection of handcrafted art pieces created by talented local artists, as well as an assortment of aromatic coffee and tea blends.
