About Coffee in Seattle

Seattle is the birthplace of coffee culture in America, home to the original Starbucks and countless independent roasters that define the Pacific Northwest coffee scene.

From cozy neighborhood spots to innovative specialty roasters, Seattle's indie coffee shops offer everything from classic espresso to experimental brewing methods.

Popular neighborhoods for coffee in Seattle include Capitol Hill, Fremont, Ballard, Queen Anne, South Lake Union, and the University District. With joe, you can order ahead from any of our 42 partner shops in Seattle, skip the line, and earn rewards on every purchase.
