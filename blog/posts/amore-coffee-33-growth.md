## 33% Revenue Growth with Mobile Ordering

Amore Coffee proved that mobile ordering isn't just about convenience - it's about growth. After implementing joe, they saw a remarkable **33% increase in revenue**.

## How They Did It

### 1. Full Team Buy-In
Amore Coffee made sure every team member understood the benefits of joe and could help customers get started with the app.

### 2. Consistent Promotion
They promoted mobile ordering through:
- In-store signage
- Social media posts
- Staff recommendations
- Email to existing customers

### 3. Leveraging the Loyalty Program
The built-in loyalty program kept customers coming back more often, driving repeat purchases and increasing lifetime value.

## The Results

After launching with joe, Amore Coffee experienced:

- **33% revenue increase**
- **Higher average tickets** on mobile orders
- **Increased customer frequency**
- **Better tips for baristas**

## The Key Insight

"We realized that mobile ordering isn't just for busy customers," says the Amore Coffee team. "It changed how our regulars engage with us. They order more often, spend more per order, and feel more connected to our shop."
