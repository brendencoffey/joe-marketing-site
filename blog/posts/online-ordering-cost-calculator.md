## The True Cost of Mobile Ordering

When evaluating mobile ordering solutions, consider:

### Upfront Costs
- Development fees
- Design costs
- Integration with your POS

### Ongoing Costs
- Monthly platform fees
- Transaction fees
- Maintenance and updates
- Marketing to drive adoption

### Hidden Costs
- Customer support
- Server hosting
- Feature updates
- Bug fixes
