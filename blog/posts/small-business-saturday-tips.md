10 easy marketing tips you can use this Small Business Saturday to maximize revenue and safely strengthen customer relationships.

## 1. Promote Early and Often

Start talking about Small Business Saturday at least a week in advance on social media and in-store signage.

## 2. Create a Special Offer

Give customers a reason to choose you on Saturday—whether it's a discount, free add-on, or special menu item.

## 3. Partner with Neighboring Businesses

Cross-promote with other local businesses to drive foot traffic to each other.

## 4. Optimize for Mobile Orders

Make sure your joe menu is up-to-date and ready for increased volume.

## 5. Share Your Story

Remind customers why supporting your shop matters—share your story on social media.

## 6. Thank Your Customers

A genuine thank you goes a long way. Consider handwritten notes or small tokens of appreciation.

## 7. Capture Contact Information

Use the opportunity to grow your customer database for future marketing.

## 8. Staff Up

Make sure you have enough team members to handle increased traffic.

## 9. Document Everything

Take photos and videos to use in future marketing and to celebrate the day.

## 10. Follow Up

After Small Business Saturday, follow up with customers to thank them and keep the momentum going.
