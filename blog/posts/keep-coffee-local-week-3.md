In this Keep Coffee Local Week 3 recap, we hear about 5 more coffee entrepreneurs and their unwavering commitment to community, and of course great coffee.

## Week 3 Highlights

The Keep Coffee Local campaign continues with five more inspiring stories of independent coffee shop owners who are making a difference in their communities.

These entrepreneurs share a common thread: they see their coffee shops as more than businesses. They're community hubs, gathering places, and sources of connection in their neighborhoods.

## Why Keep Coffee Local Matters

When you choose local coffee, you're:

- Supporting local entrepreneurs and job creation
- Keeping money circulating in your community
- Preserving unique neighborhood character
- Getting better coffee from people who care
