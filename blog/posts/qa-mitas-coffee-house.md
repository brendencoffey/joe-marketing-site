## Meet Mita's Coffee House

We sat down with April Pomeroy, owner of Mita's Coffee House, to learn about her journey in coffee and how joe has helped grow her business.

Mita's Coffee House is more than just a coffee shop - it's a gathering place for the community. April has built a loyal following by focusing on quality coffee, genuine hospitality, and creating a welcoming space for everyone.

## The joe Difference

Since implementing joe, Mita's has seen:

- Increased customer loyalty through the built-in rewards program
- Higher order frequency from regular customers
- Reduced wait times during peak hours
- Better customer data for targeted promotions

## April's Advice for Fellow Shop Owners

"Don't be afraid to embrace technology that helps you serve your customers better. joe made it easy for us to offer mobile ordering without losing the personal touch that makes our shop special."
