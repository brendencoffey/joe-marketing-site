A look at the data surrounding COVID-19, how it's changing consumer behavior, and what new opportunities are emerging as a result for coffee shops.

## The New Reality

The pandemic has fundamentally changed how consumers interact with coffee shops. Understanding these changes is crucial for adapting and thriving.

## Key Trends

- **Accelerated mobile adoption** - Customers are more comfortable than ever ordering digitally
- **Safety-first mindset** - Contactless options are no longer nice-to-have
- **Support for local** - Consumers want to help their community businesses survive
- **Changed commute patterns** - Work-from-home has shifted when and where people get coffee

## Opportunities for Coffee Shops

- Embrace mobile ordering and curbside pickup
- Communicate safety measures clearly
- Build relationships with customers digitally
- Adapt hours and offerings to new patterns
