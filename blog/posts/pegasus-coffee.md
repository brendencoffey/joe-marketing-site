## Never Settle for Coffee You Don't Love

Pegasus Coffee in Seattle, Washington has been serving the Bainbridge Island community with exceptional coffee for years. Now, with joe, their customers never have to settle for less - even when they're in a hurry.

## The Perfect Partnership

Pegasus Coffee understands that their customers come for the quality and the experience. But they also know that life gets busy. With joe, their regulars can:

- Order their favorite drinks ahead of time
- Skip the line during busy ferry commute times
- Earn rewards with every purchase
- Never miss their morning coffee, even on hectic days

## Supporting Local

When you order through joe at Pegasus Coffee, you're supporting a local business that pours their heart into every cup. Unlike third-party delivery apps that take huge commissions, joe keeps more money in the hands of the shop owners and baristas who make your coffee special.

## A Seattle Tradition, Now Mobile

Pegasus Coffee represents the best of Pacific Northwest coffee culture - quality, community, and a commitment to craft. With joe, they're bringing that tradition into the mobile age without losing what makes them special.
