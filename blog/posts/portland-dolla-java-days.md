Dolla Java Days in Portland, OR kicks off April 12 through 25, 2021. For two weeks, we're sponsoring $1 coffee and drinks for Portland joe customers!

## Portland Coffee Culture

Portland has one of the most vibrant independent coffee scenes in the country. From innovative roasters to cozy neighborhood shops, the city is a coffee lover's paradise.

## How to Participate

1. Download the joe app
2. Find participating Portland coffee shops
3. Enjoy $1 coffee for two weeks!

## Supporting Local

This promotion is designed to help Portland coffee shops gain new customers while giving coffee lovers an affordable way to explore the city's incredible independent coffee scene.
