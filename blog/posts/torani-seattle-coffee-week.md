Like-minded companies joe and Torani Syrups are teaming up for a promotional event all about celebrating local Seattle coffee.

## A Perfect Partnership

Torani has been making premium syrups and sauces since 1925, and their commitment to quality aligns perfectly with joe's mission to support independent coffee shops.

## Celebrating Local Coffee

Seattle Coffee Week is all about highlighting the incredible independent coffee shops that make Seattle's coffee scene world-renowned. Together, joe and Torani are bringing special promotions and experiences to coffee lovers across the city.

Stay tuned for more details on how you can participate and support your favorite local coffee shops during Seattle Coffee Week!
