Seattle Coffee Week, October 1 - 14, celebrates craft, community, resilience, and delicious Seattle coffee!

## Two Weeks of Coffee Celebration

Seattle Coffee Week is the ultimate celebration of the city's legendary coffee culture. From October 1st through the 14th, coffee lovers can explore new shops, enjoy special promotions, and support independent coffee.

## $1 Coffee for New Users

New to joe? During Seattle Coffee Week, new users in the Seattle area can enjoy $1 coffee at participating shops. It's the perfect opportunity to discover your new favorite local coffee spot.

## How to Participate

1. Download the joe app
2. Find participating Seattle Coffee Week shops
3. Order ahead and enjoy!

Join us in celebrating Seattle's incredible independent coffee scene!
