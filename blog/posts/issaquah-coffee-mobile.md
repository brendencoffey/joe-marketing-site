Issaquah Coffee Company saw such success with joe's mobile ordering that they made a significant investment in their operations.

## The Challenge

With mobile orders growing rapidly, Issaquah Coffee Company needed to:
- Handle increased mobile order volume
- Keep in-person customers happy
- Maintain efficient barista workflow

## The Solution

Rather than let mobile orders slow down their operations, they added a second espresso machine dedicated to mobile orders.

## The Results

This strategic move allowed Issaquah Coffee Company to:
- Prioritize mobile order customers with dedicated equipment
- Keep in-person service fast and friendly
- Make barista workflow more efficient

The investment paid off, with mobile orders continuing to grow while in-person service remained excellent.
