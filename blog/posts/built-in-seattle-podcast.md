"Built in Seattle with Adam Schoenfeld" invited joe CEO and Co-founder Nick Martin to the podcast. Listen in to hear his journey to connecting with coffee and building a company that supports independent coffee shops.

## About the Episode

Nick shares the story of how joe was founded, the challenges of building a company in the coffee industry, and the vision for helping independent coffee shops thrive in an increasingly competitive landscape.

## Key Takeaways

- The inspiration behind starting joe
- How technology can level the playing field for independent coffee shops
- The importance of community in coffee culture
- joe's vision for the future of independent coffee

Check out the full episode to hear Nick's story and learn more about the mission behind joe.
