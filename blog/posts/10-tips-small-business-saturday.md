Small Business Saturday is one of the biggest opportunities of the year for independent coffee shops. Here are 10 actionable tips to make the most of it.

## Before the Day

### 1. Promote Early and Often

Start building buzz at least a week before:
- Post on social media daily
- Send an email to your customer list
- Put up signage in your shop
- Partner with nearby businesses to cross-promote

### 2. Create a Special Offer

Give customers a reason to choose you on Saturday:
- Exclusive Small Business Saturday drink
- Bonus loyalty points
- Free pastry with purchase
- Limited edition merchandise

### 3. Prepare Your Team

Make sure everyone is ready:
- Brief staff on promotions
- Review expected busy times
- Ensure adequate staffing
- Prep extra supplies

## During the Day

### 4. Make It an Experience

Transform your shop into something special:
- Play festive music
- Decorate for the season
- Offer samples of seasonal drinks
- Create photo opportunities

### 5. Encourage Social Sharing

Turn customers into promoters:
- Create a branded hashtag
- Offer incentives for social posts
- Display a "selfie spot"
- Repost customer content

### 6. Capture New Customers

First-time visitors are gold:
- Have loyalty sign-up materials ready
- Collect email addresses
- Give a reason to return (bounce-back offer)
- Make a great first impression

### 7. Upsell Thoughtfully

Increase average ticket without being pushy:
- Suggest add-ons naturally
- Promote gift cards for holiday giving
- Highlight seasonal specialties
- Bundle deals for value

## After the Day

### 8. Follow Up Quickly

Strike while the iron is hot:
- Send thank you emails within 48 hours
- Share photos from the day on social
- Announce how much was raised (if doing a giveback)
- Tease upcoming holiday offerings

### 9. Analyze What Worked

Learn for next year:
- Track sales by hour
- Note which promotions drove traffic
- Gather staff feedback
- Survey customers

### 10. Keep the Momentum

Don't let the energy die:
- Continue promotions through the holidays
- Remind new customers about loyalty rewards
- Plan your next community event
- Thank your team
