Indaba Coffee was one of joe's early partners, seeing the value of being part of a nationwide network of independent coffee shops.

## The Journey

Indaba Coffee recognized early on that independent coffee shops are stronger together. By joining joe, they became part of something bigger than just their own shop.

## Navigating the Pandemic

When the pandemic hit, Indaba Coffee had already built:
- A loyal customer base through joe's rewards
- Mobile ordering capabilities
- Digital relationships with customers

These tools helped them survive and even grow during one of the most challenging times in the industry's history.

## The Results

As an early adopter, Indaba Coffee has:
- Built lasting customer relationships
- Grown despite global challenges
- Proven the value of being part of an indie coffee network

Their journey shows that investing in the right technology early pays dividends for years to come.
