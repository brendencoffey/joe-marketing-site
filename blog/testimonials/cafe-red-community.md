Cafe Red is more than just a coffee shop—it's a community anchor in South Seattle.

## About Cafe Red

Located in South Seattle, Cafe Red offers:
- Amazing coffee
- Delicious food options
- A welcoming space for the community

## A Commitment to Community

Cafe Red's mission goes beyond serving great coffee. They're committed to:
- Supporting their local community
- Creating an inclusive space
- Building lasting relationships with customers

## Partnering with joe

By accepting mobile order and pickup through joe, Cafe Red has been able to:
- Offer modern convenience to customers
- Reach new customers through the joe network
- Focus on what they do best—community and coffee

Cafe Red proves that independent coffee shops can embrace technology while staying true to their community-focused roots.

— Team, Cafe Red
