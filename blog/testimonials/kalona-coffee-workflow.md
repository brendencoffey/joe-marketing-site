Kalona Coffee House found the perfect balance between their existing Square setup and the powerful features of joe's mobile ordering and loyalty platform.

## The Challenge

Kalona Coffee House was using Square for their core operations but needed:
- Better mobile ordering capabilities
- Automated loyalty marketing
- A way to maximize workflow efficiency

## The Solution: joe + Square Integration

By integrating joe with their existing Square system, Kalona Coffee House was able to:
- Keep their familiar Square setup
- Add joe's powerful mobile ordering
- Leverage automated loyalty marketing

## The Results

With joe + Square integration, Kalona Coffee House has:
- Maximized their barista workflow
- Better served their customers with mobile ordering
- Opened up new opportunities for growth

The seamless integration means they get the best of both worlds—Square's reliability and joe's coffee-specific features.

— Owner, Kalona Coffee House
