Vanessa Alvarez, owner of Catalina's Coffee & Cocktails in Sunnyside, Washington, has built more than just a coffee shop—she's built a community hub.

With a background that includes roles at the University of Washington and Redfin, Vanessa has always been passionate about creating inclusive environments. This value is at the heart of Catalina's Coffee & Cocktails.

## The Challenge

Transitioning from a structured corporate environment to the dynamic world of coffee entrepreneurship came with its own set of challenges. Vanessa needed technology that would support her vision without getting in the way.

## The Solution

joe Point of Sale gave Catalina's Coffee the tools to:
- Streamline operations
- Build customer loyalty
- Focus on what matters most—service and community

## The Results

With joe Point of Sale, Catalina's Coffee & Cocktails has been able to:
- Grow their customer base through automated loyalty and rewards
- Create an inclusive, welcoming environment
- Focus on community-building rather than technology management

Vanessa continues to consult and run workshops for organizations like the City of Seattle to help create more inclusive work environments, all while running a thriving coffee business.

— Vanessa Alvarez, Owner, Catalina's Coffee & Cocktails
